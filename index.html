The Bechdel Test

The Bechdel test originated in this comic, created by the cartoonist Alison Bechdel, encapsulating ideas she credits to her friend Liz Wallace. It has three components.

  1. The film contains at least two named women.
  2. These women have a conversation on screen.
  3. The conversation is about something other than a man.

Although these requirements seem simple, many movies fail the Bechdel Test. Some don't even meet the first condition.
